Our Story

Established in 2015

T Elshadai Clinic and Maternity was founded in 2015 by Mrs. Adewale Morenikeji Victoria with a vision to provide quality healthcare services to the community. What started as a small clinic has grown into one of the region's most trusted healthcare institutions.

Over the past 10+ years, we have continuously expanded our facilities, adopted modern medical technology, and assembled a team of qualified healthcare professionals. Our journey has been marked by unwavering dedication to patient care, medical excellence, and community service.

Today, T Elshadai Clinic and Maternity stands as a beacon of hope and healing, serving thousands of patients annually. Our commitment to accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare remains as strong as it was on day one.

Meet Our Founder

Mrs. Adewale Morenikeji Victoria

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Mrs. Adewale Morenikeji Victoria is a visionary healthcare leader with a passion for community health. With her leadership, T Elshadai Clinic and Maternity has become a trusted name in healthcare delivery in Ogun State and beyond.

Under her visionary leadership, the clinic has received numerous accolades and continues to expand its services to meet the growing healthcare needs of the community. She is committed to ensuring that quality healthcare is accessible to all.

Her philosophy is simple: "Every patient deserves compassionate care delivered with clinical excellence." This principle continues to guide every aspect of our clinic's operations.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare services to all members of our community, ensuring every patient receives personalized attention, compassionate care, and treatment that meets the highest international standards of medical excellence.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare institution in West Africa, recognized for clinical excellence, innovative medical solutions, patient-centered care, and our contribution to medical education and research, while maintaining our commitment to serving all members of society.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: Treating every patient with empathy and dignity
  • Excellence: Maintaining the highest standards in all we do
  • Integrity: Operating with honesty and transparency
  • Innovation: Embracing new technologies and methods
  • Teamwork: Collaborating for better patient outcomes
