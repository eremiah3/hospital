  • Parents/Guardians: 24-hour access

Visitor Guidelines

  • Visitors must check in at the reception desk
  • Valid ID required for all visitors
  • Hand sanitization is mandatory before entering wards
  • Children under 12 years are not permitted (except pediatric ward)
  • Visitors showing signs of illness should not visit
  • Maintain quiet environment in all wards
  • Mobile phones should be on silent mode
  • No smoking or alcohol anywhere on hospital premises
  • Follow all instructions from hospital staff
