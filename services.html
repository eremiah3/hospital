Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions for all your medical needs

Comprehensive Medical Services

At T Elshadai Clinic and Maternity, we offer a range of medical services. Our facilities, combined with our team of experienced healthcare professionals, ensure you receive the highest quality care for all your health needs.

Child and Maternal Health

Our Child and Maternal Health department is dedicated to providing comprehensive healthcare for mothers and children. We offer a nurturing environment with experienced specialists who ensure the health and well-being of both mother and child.

Services Include:

  • Prenatal care and monitoring
  • Safe delivery services
  • Postnatal care for mother and baby
  • Well-child clinics
  • Growth and development monitoring
  • Nutritional counseling for mothers and children
  • Mother support groups

Availability: 24/7

Antenatal Care

Our Antenatal Care program provides comprehensive care for expectant mothers throughout their pregnancy journey. We monitor both mother and baby's health to ensure a safe and healthy pregnancy.

Services Include:

  • Regular prenatal checkups
  • Ultrasound scans and fetal monitoring
  • Blood pressure and sugar monitoring
  • Vaccinations during pregnancy
  • Nutritional counseling
  • Birth preparation classes
  • High-risk pregnancy management

Consultation Hours: Monday - Saturday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Postnatal Care

Our Postnatal Care services ensure the health and recovery of mothers after childbirth, as well as the well-being of newborns. We provide support and guidance during this crucial period.

Services Include:

  • Postpartum checkups for mothers
  • Newborn screening and examinations
  • Breastfeeding support and counseling
  • Family planning advice
  • Mental health support for postpartum depression
  • Vaccination schedules for newborns

Availability: 24/7

Infant Immunization

Our Immunization program protects infants and children from various preventable diseases. We follow the national immunization schedule to ensure children receive all necessary vaccines.

Services Include:

  • BCG and Polio vaccines
  • Pentavalent vaccines
  • Measles and Rubella vaccines
  • Yellow fever vaccination
  • Vitamin A supplementation
  • Growth monitoring
  • Immunization records and cards

Clinic Hours: Monday - Saturday, 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Primary Health Care

Our Primary Health Care services provide comprehensive and continuous healthcare for patients of all ages. We focus on preventive care, health promotion, and management of common illnesses.

Services Include:

  • General medical consultations
  • Preventive health screenings
  • Management of chronic diseases (diabetes, hypertension)
  • Treatment of common illnesses
  • Health education and counseling
  • Referral services to specialists
  • Home care services

Availability: 24/7

Counselling

Our Counseling services provide professional support for individuals and families facing various health and personal challenges. Our trained counselors offer a safe and confidential environment.

Services Include:

  • Pre-marital counseling
  • Family planning counseling
  • Mental health counseling
  • HIV/AIDS counseling and testing
  • Nutritional counseling
  • Stress management
  • Postpartum depression support

Consultation Hours: Monday - Saturday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Health Education

Our Health Education program aims to empower individuals and communities with knowledge to make informed decisions about their health. We conduct various educational sessions and programs.

Topics Covered:

  • Maternal and child health
  • Nutrition and healthy lifestyle
  • Disease prevention and control
  • Family planning methods
  • Personal hygiene
  • Communicable disease awareness
  • Community health programs

Programs: Daily during clinic hours and community outreach

Medical Outreach

Our Medical Outreach program brings healthcare services to underserved communities. We organize regular outreach programs to ensure everyone has access to quality healthcare.

Outreach Activities:

  • Free medical checkups
  • Health screenings
  • Vaccination drives
  • Health education campaigns
  • Distribution of health materials
  • Referral services for specialized care

Schedule: Monthly community visits

Home Care Services

Our Home Care Services bring quality healthcare to the comfort of our patients' homes. We provide professional medical care for patients who require ongoing attention but prefer to stay at home.

Services Include:

  • Home visits by nurses and doctors
  • Wound care and dressing
  • Injection and medication administration
  • Patient monitoring
  • Post-surgical care
  • Elderly care services
  • Physiotherapy at home

Availability: By appointment

Contact: 08057427115

Family Planning

Our Family Planning services help individuals and couples make informed decisions about their reproductive health. We offer a range of contraceptive methods and counseling.

Services Include:

  • Contraceptive counseling
  • Oral contraceptives
  • Injectable contraceptives
  • Intrauterine devices (IUD)
  • Implanon/Norplant insertion
  • Fertility awareness education
  • Pre-conception counseling

Consultation Hours: Monday - Saturday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
