Welcome to T Elshadai Clinic and Maternity

Established in 2015

T Elshadai Clinic and Maternity has been a cornerstone of healthcare excellence in our community for over 10 years. Founded by Mrs. Adewale Morenikeji Victoria, our clinic combines quality medical technology with compassionate care.

Our mission is to provide accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare to all members of our community, ensuring every patient receives personalized attention and the highest standard of medical care.
